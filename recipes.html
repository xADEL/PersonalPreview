Kuwaiti Chicken Machboos

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 large carrot, grated
  • 1 large potato, diced
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground black lime (loomi) or use lime zest
  • 2-3 dried black limes (loomi)
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for about 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat vegetable oil and ghee over medium heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add chicken pieces to the pot and season with cumin, coriander, cinnamon, cardamom, turmeric, ground black lime, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  5. Cook the chicken until browned on all sides.
  6. Add grated carrot and diced potatoes. Stir and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Pour in chicken broth and add dried black limes. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  8. Cover the pot and let it simmer until the chicken is tender.
  9. Meanwhile, in a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add salt and the soaked, drained rice. Cook until almost done.
  10. Drain the rice and add it to the pot with the chicken. Mix gently to combine.
  11. Cover the pot and let it cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and absorbs the flavors.
  12. Once done, fluff the rice with a fork. Serve hot, garnished with fried onions if desired.

Ramen

Ingredients:

  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green onion, chopped (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Boiled eggs (optional, for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pot, bring the chicken or vegetable broth to a simmer.
  3. Add sliced mushrooms, baby spinach, soy sauce, miso paste, sesame oil, grated ginger, and minced garlic to the pot. Simmer for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Divide the cooked ramen noodles into serving bowls.
  5. Ladle the hot broth with vegetables over the noodles.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions, sesame seeds, and boiled eggs if desired.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious bowl of ramen!

Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Maple syrup for serving
  • Fresh berries (optional, for topping)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. It's okay if there are some lumps.
  4. Heat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  8. Serve the pancakes warm with maple syrup and fresh berries if desired.
